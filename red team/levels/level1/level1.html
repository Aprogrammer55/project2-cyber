Reconnaissance (Recon) is the first and most critical phase in ethical hacking and red teaming. It involves gathering as much information as possible about a target before attempting any exploitation.

There are two main types of recon:

1. Passive Recon: Collecting information without directly interacting with the target. Examples: Google Dorking, WHOIS lookup, social media, breached data, public records.

2. Active Recon: Directly interacting with the target to obtain data. Examples: port scanning, service enumeration, OS detection.

A strong recon phase makes later attacks easier and more effective. You gather intel first ‚Äî you never attack blindly.
