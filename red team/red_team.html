What is Red Teaming?

Simulate attackers to improve real-world defenses

Red teams use offensive techniques (recon, exploitation, lateral movement, persistence) to test how well an organization detects and responds to sophisticated attacks. This site gives you the basics and safe exercises to practice.

Recon

Discover targets and gather public info — the first step in every engagement.

Exploitation

Use weaknesses to gain access (always in lab or authorized scope).

Post-Exploitation

Move laterally, maintain access, and collect evidence for defenders to fix issues.

Quick facts

Red teams help blue teams (defenders) by revealing gaps. They operate ethically — with permission and clear rules.
