Cyber Security — Red Team & Blue Team

Red Team
Mission

The Red Team simulates real-world cyber attacks to test an organization’s defenses. Their goal is to identify weaknesses before real attackers do.

Core Roles

  • Red Team Operator (Exploitation & Payloads)
  • Threat Emulator (Adversary TTP mapping)
  • OPSEC Specialist (Stealth & Evasion)

Skills Required

Penetration Testing Lateral Movement Privilege Escalation C2 Frameworks Social Engineering

Tools

  • Cobalt Strike / Sliver
  • Metasploit Framework
  • Burp Suite
  • BloodHound
  • Custom Payloads & Scripts
Blue Team
Mission

The Blue Team defends the organization’s network by monitoring, detecting, and responding to cyber threats in real-time.

Core Roles

  • SOC Analyst (Monitoring & Alerting)
  • Incident Responder
  • Threat Hunter
  • Forensics Specialist

Skills Required

SIEM Monitoring Log Analysis Threat Intelligence Incident Response Digital Forensics

Tools

  • Splunk / QRadar / ELK
  • EDR (CrowdStrike, Defender, SentinelOne)
  • Wireshark
  • OSINT Tools
  • Forensics Suites
